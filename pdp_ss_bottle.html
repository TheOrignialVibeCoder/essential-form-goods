Discover the essence of refined hydration with the Essential Form Goods Insulated Stainless Steel Bottle line. True to our 'Quality Unseen. Quality Felt.' ethos, each bottle in this collection is a study in minimalist design and enduring performance. Crafted from premium 18/8 food-grade stainless steel, these bottles feature advanced double-wall vacuum insulation, keeping your beverages refreshingly cold for up to 24 hours or perfectly hot for up to 12 hours. The sleek, unadorned form offers a comfortable grip and timeless appeal, designed to seamlessly integrate into your daily life. Whether you choose a compact size for your commute or a larger capacity for extended adventures, each bottle promises reliability and a pure taste experience. This line represents our commitment to quality that is both subtle and deeply satisfying.

  • Insulation: Double-wall vacuum insulation.
  • Capacity Range: Available in various capacities to suit your needs (e.g., 18oz, 24oz, 32oz).
  • Dimensions: Varies by capacity. Designed for comfortable handling and portability.
